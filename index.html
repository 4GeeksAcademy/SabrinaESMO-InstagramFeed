Mistborn

09/23

As book lover, finding the work of Brandon Sanderson made me live a before and after. His stories are so original and well written, but what I like the most is the way he builds the magic laws on his worlds. Normally, the characters who get some kind of power are those who were broken at some point, depressed or damaged.

Sant Jordi 2023

04/23

In Catalunya, on April 23rd we celebrate Sant Jordi filling the streets with roses and books. It is my favourite festivity, one that comes from a legend that involves a princess, a prince and a dragon. I really love how that day, the streets of my city (and all other around Catalonia) are full of people admiring and sharing their love for literature.
